## Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Create Your Stripe Account

1. **Go to Stripe's website:** Visit [https://stripe.com](https://stripe.com)

2. **Click "Start now" or "Sign up"** in the top right corner

3. **Enter your email address** and create a strong password

4. **Verify your email:** Check your inbox for a verification email from Stripe and click the link

### Step 2: Choose Account Type

1. **Select "Brazil" as your country** (or your organization's registered country)

2. **Choose "Company" as the account type** (not "Individual")

3. **Select your organization type:**
   - Nonprofit
   - NGO (Non-Governmental Organization)
   - Foundation
   - Association
   - Or the closest match to your legal structure

### Step 3: Enter Organization Details

Fill in your organization information:

1. **Legal business name:** Enter your organization's full legal name exactly as it appears on registration documents

2. **Tax ID:** Enter your organization's tax identification number
   - Brazil: CNPJ (Cadastro Nacional da Pessoa Jur√≠dica)
   - Other countries: equivalent tax ID

3. **Business address:** Enter your organization's registered address

4. **Phone number:** A contact number where Stripe can reach your organization

5. **Website:** Your organization's official website

6. **Industry:** Select "Nonprofit" or "Environmental Services" or the most appropriate category

### Step 4: Enter Representative Information

Stripe requires information about the person authorized to represent your organization:

1. **Full name:** Legal name of the authorized representative

2. **Date of birth:** For identity verification

3. **Email & Phone:** Contact details for this person

4. **Home address:** Personal address of the representative

5. **Government ID:** You'll need to upload a photo of a valid government-issued ID
   - Passport (recommended for international verification)
   - National ID card
   - Driver's license

### Step 5: Add Banking Information

This is where Habitat English donations will be deposited:

1. **Bank country:** Select your bank's country

2. **Currency:** Select BRL (Brazilian Real) or your local currency

3. **Bank account details:**
   - Bank name
   - Account holder name (must match your organization name)
   - Account number
   - Routing number (for Brazil: Agency and Account number)

4. **Account type:** Usually "Checking" for organizations

**Important:** Double-check all banking details. Incorrect information will delay payments.

### Step 6: Verification Documents

Stripe may request additional documents for verification:

1. **Proof of organization registration:**
   - Certificate of incorporation
   - NGO registration certificate
   - Tax-exempt documentation

2. **Proof of address:**
   - Recent utility bill
   - Bank statement
   - Government letter
   - Must be less than 3 months old

3. **Bank verification:**
   - Bank statement showing account details
   - Void check or bank letter

**Tip:** Have these ready as PDFs or clear photos before you start.

### Step 7: Enable Stripe Connect

This is how Habitat English will transfer donations to you:

1. Habitat English will send you a **Stripe Connect invitation link** via email

2. Click the link in the email

3. Sign in to your Stripe account

4. Review the connection details

5. Click **"Authorize"** to allow Habitat English to send you transfers

**Important:** Do NOT skip this step - without connecting, we cannot send you donations.

### Step 8: Verification Process

After submitting your information:

1. **Stripe will review your application** (usually 2-7 business days)

2. **You may receive requests for additional information:**
   - Check your email regularly
   - Respond promptly to avoid delays

3. **Once verified, you'll receive a confirmation email**

4. **Notify Habitat English** that your account is verified and ready

## What Happens Next?

### Donation Schedule

- **Frequency:** Monthly transfers
- **Timing:** First week of each month
- **Amount:** Your share of the donation percentage from the previous month's subscriptions
- **Currency:** Donations will be in BRL (Brazilian Real) to avoid conversion fees

### How You'll Receive Payments

1. Habitat English subscribers make payments ‚Üí our Stripe account

2. Our system automatically calculates the donation percentage

3. At the end of each month, donations are split among partner NGOs

4. First week of the following month: automatic transfer to your Stripe account

5. Stripe deposits funds to your bank account (typically 2-5 business days)

### Transparency & Reporting

You'll have access to:
- **Stripe Dashboard:** View all incoming transfers
- **Monthly statements:** Detailed breakdown of donations received
- **Annual reports:** Habitat English will provide annual summaries for your records

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

**‚ùå "We couldn't verify your organization"**
- Solution: Upload additional documentation (registration certificate, tax docs)
- Make sure all documents are clear, in color, and less than 3 months old

**‚ùå "Banking information doesn't match"**
- Solution: Ensure bank account is in the organization's name, not a personal account

**‚ùå "ID verification failed"**

- Notify Habitat English if delays exceed 2 weeks
