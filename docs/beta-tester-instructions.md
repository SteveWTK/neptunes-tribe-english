## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long is my beta access valid?
Your premium access continues as long as you remain an active beta tester with your partner organization.

### Can I share my invitation code?
No, invitation codes are **one-time use only**. Each person needs their own code from their organization coordinator.

### What if my code doesn't work?
- Make sure you typed it correctly (copy/paste is best)
- Check with your organization coordinator
- Contact Habitat English support

### Can my family use it too?
Beta access is individual. Family members can sign up for Habitat English, but they would need their own invitation code or a regular subscription.

### How often should I give feedback?
As often as you like! We appreciate:
- Quick feedback after each session (optional)
- Detailed feedback once a week or month
- Immediate reports for any bugs or problems

### Will my feedback be public?
No, your feedback is private and only visible to Habitat English administrators and your organization coordinator.

### What happens to my account after beta testing?
This depends on your organization's partnership agreement. We'll communicate any changes well in advance.
